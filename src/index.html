Water Systems

This web map shows Water System locations and boundaries in San Juan County WA. Water system boundaries are defined by the parcels included in the water system as set in the San Juan County Health Department's database.

How to Use

Search for your water system name, address or parcel number. Click on a marker or boundary for information related to your water system.

Please report mapping errors to sjcgis@sanjuanco.com

Select a water system boundary on the map to see more information.
